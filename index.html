Cosmic Clause

To all the creative and talented writers out there, pick up your pen and get ready. In collaboration with Panacea, English literary society of IIT Bhubaneswar, and Abhivyakti, Hindi literary society of IIT Bhubaneswar, we are organizing two essay writing competitions, one for English and the other for Hindi. Do participate and present your ideas to whole India!

THEME:
1. Compose a substitute closure for any cosmic film of your advantage.
2. Express your considerations about the paradox of 9th planet in our Solar System.

Date: 21st May to 6th June, 2021.

Reward: “Certificate of Excellence” to top three participants. Also, their names will be highlighted on Instagram for at least a week!

RULES:
1. Choose anyone of the two themes given above and write an article / essay on it.
2. Submit your entries as .pdf or word document (typed only)

For submissions, send your entries to: secylitsoc.sg@iitbbs.ac.in
The subject of your email should be of the format: [Your name] [Your college name] [The topic of your article/essay]
Your email should contain your details (Your name, contact number (preferably Whatsapp), your Institution name, and your Instagram ID (if any)).

DEADLINE: 6th June, 2021. (Deadline has been extended).

In case of any issue, please contact on the email ID given above.

The results will be declared on this website and on the Instagram handles of Nakshatra and Panacea on Evening of 13th June, 2021.

*The Rules are subject to change. Nakshatra and the collaborating society (if any) will have the final decision on the competition (including the results).

Astrophotography

For all the photography enthusiasts out there, Nakshatra has organised an astrophotography competition. So grab your cameras and get ready to flaunt your skills. The photographs can be captured using a digital camera or a smartphone.

Do take part and capture the beauty of the cosmos!

Further, we want to thank our event partner Astrophotography India for their valuable contribution in this competition.

Theme: “Elements of The Night Sky”
As the theme suggests, the photograph submitted should have elements of the night sky as the main subject of the photograph. This can include a wide variety of styles and interpretations. It is recommended and encouraged that the images be framed around the genre of astrophotography. However, photography being a creative endeavour, any other type of image with elements from the night sky are also welcome.

Date: 21st May to 6th June, 2021.

Reward: “Certificate of Excellence” to top three participants. Also, their names will be highlighted on Instagram for at least a week!

RULES:
1. Photographs can be captured using a digital camera or smartphone.
2. Multiple exposures are allowed.
3. Shutter speed, aperture, ISO and focal length need to be provided during submission. EXIF data embedded in the submitted file is also acceptable.
4. Post processing including stacking and blending of images is allowed.
5. Star trail photography is allowed.
6. Resolution of the photograph submitted should not be less than 2 megapixels and not more than 30 megapixels.
7. File format for submission is JPEG.
8. Only 1 entry per person is allowed. No multiple entries.
9. The form will accept responses till 12:00 midnight, 31st May, 2021.

JUDGING CRITERIA:
1. Every entry will be marked out of 10 marks in the following fashion:
5 marks for the composition
5 marks for the image quality
2. It is to be noted that any number of marks out of 10 (including fractional values) can be awarded to a participant.
3. The photographs submitted by the participants need to be taken by them. In case it is found that the photograph submitted by the participant has been taken by someone else, the concerned participant will be disqualified.
